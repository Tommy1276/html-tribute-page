About Me

I'm a Web Developer based in New York

Hey there! I’m Om Prakash, a code-slinging, bug-busting educator with over 3 years of experience in training and development. If you’re here to learn C, C++, Java, or DSA—or just want to know why your code throws tantrums—I’m your go-to person.I kicked off my career at FacePrep, where I trained like a Jedi apprentice in the ways of tech. Later, I joined PrepBytes as a core team member, publishing articles, crafting UI, and helping build out the PrepBytes website—making the frontend smooth enough to charm even the pickiest browser. Along the way, I’ve trained over 20000+ students in tech that actually works, covering everything from Full Stack Development and Networking to Linux OS (because hey, someone’s got to make Linux fun, right?).With a B.Sc. in Computer Science** and an MBA in IT and Project Management, I’m not just here to teach code; I’m here to give you the big-picture approach. When I’m not deep into dev work or training, you can find me cooking up a storm or singing like nobody’s listening (thankfully, I’m a better singer than a debugger). Ready to dive into the world of tech with a dash of humor? Let’s make coding as thrilling as a plot twist—and maybe just a bit more predictable. This intro keeps it light and relatable, perfect for setting a friendly tone with your students!

I'm passionate about clean code, intuitive design, and creating seamless user experiences that drive results. Whether you need a simple landing page or a complex web application, I can bring your vision to life.
